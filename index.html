About Kappa Eta Kappa

Kappa Eta Kappa is a co-ed professional fraternity dedicated to promoting excellence in engineering and computer science. Since our founding, we have been committed to developing future leaders through professional growth and meaningful relationships.

While our membership is primarily composed of Engineering, Computer Science, and Data Science majors, we welcome students from all disciplines who share our commitment to professional excellence and community.

Our Delta Chapter at UW-Madison is proud to serve the campus community through professional development, social engagement, and lifelong friendships.
